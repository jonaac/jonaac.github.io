I participated in the RBCI NGI Challange, we formed a team with developers, buisness students adn designers. We were given a challenge theme; "Build an innovative prototype of a mobile, tablet, or web app that RBC could offer to help business owners start, manage, and grow their small businesses."

Our solution, RBC Athene:
A web app that provided a single environment where business owners could access all the tools and information available from RBC.The application would combine data analytics and machine learning to create an environment that provided the best support possible for each user. We used Javascript frameworks and libraries (Node.Js, Express.Js and jQuery), CSS (Bootstrap) and HTML.

Our team was selected as finalists (top 5) out of 35+ teams that participated.
